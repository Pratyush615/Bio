Red Blood Cells are a type of blood cell that is made in the bone marrow and found in the blood. Red blood cells contain a protein called hemoglobin, which carries oxygen from the lungs to all parts of the body.

Sickle Cell Disease is a genetic condition that is present at birth. It is inherited when a child receives two sickle cell genes; one from each parent.
